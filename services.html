Safe Deposit Lockers

Guard your valuables — jewellery, important documents, and precious items — with complete peace of mind in our highly secure, insured lockers available across our branches.

  • High-security steel lockers
  • Multiple size options
  • 24/7 security surveillance
  • Available across branches

Debit Cards

Access your funds anytime, anywhere with our secure RuPay/ATM debit cards. Shop online, at stores, or withdraw cash from any ATM across India with ease.

  • RuPay enabled debit cards
  • ATM Cash Withdrawal
  • Online & POS transactions
  • UPI linked payments

Digital Banking & UPI

Pay bills, transfer funds, and manage finances digitally through all major UPI payment platforms. Mahaveer Bank supports BHIM, Paytm, Google Pay, PhonePe & WhatsApp Pay.

  • BHIM, Paytm, Google Pay
  • WhatsApp Pay & PhonePe
  • Instant fund transfers
  • Bill payment services

Utility Bill Payments

Pay all your utility bills conveniently through our digital channels or at any branch — electricity, gas, water, broadband, DTH, mobile recharge, school/college fees.

  • Electricity & Water bills
  • Gas & Broadband
  • DTH & Mobile recharge
  • School / College fees

NEFT / RTGS / IMPS

Transfer money to any bank account in India instantly with IMPS, or on same-day basis through NEFT and RTGS. Reliable, fast, and secure fund transfers.

  • NEFT – Same day transfers
  • RTGS – Real time large value
  • IMPS – Instant 24×7 transfer
  • Nominal charges

Passbook & Statements

Keep track of every transaction with regular passbook updates and account statements. Available at any branch or on request for all savings and current account holders.

  • Regular passbook updates
  • Printed account statements
  • Transaction history
  • Available at all branches
